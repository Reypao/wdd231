About Me

Professionally, I navigate the technical demands of water irrigation systems as a technician and inside-sales to contractor serving West Jordan, UT. This hands-on role has honed my ability to diagnose issues, implement practical solutions, and understand client needs. I'm now channeling that dedication into a new chapter, actively pursuing a software development education with BYU Pathway Worldwide to build innovative solutions in the tech landscape. At the heart of everything I do is my family, I'm a committed husband and a proud dad to three incredible triplet girls, who teach me resilience and perspective every day. My interests provide balance and inspiration. Drawing allows me a creative outlet, while movies offer escape and storytelling. Tennis keeps me active and sharpens my strategic thinking. I'm naturally curious and love discovering new places, which fuels my understanding of diverse needs and solutions. Ultimately, whether it's optimizing a sprinkler system, writing clean code, or simply lending a hand, I'm driven by a desire to apply my expertise to make a tangible, positive difference for people.
